Course Package Description

This Travel & Learn course package includes the following three courses.

1. My Office Burnt Down. Are You Prepared?

Speaker: Dr. Maria Colaiacovo
3 CE points

Course Description

A fire in a dental office can have devastating consequences, including interruption of patient care, damage to specialized equipment, loss of records, regulatory challenges and significant financial impact. This course is designed to provide dentist and practice owners with the knowledge and tools necessary to proactively prepare for, respond to and recover from a dental office fire or other catastrophic event.

Participants will gain an understanding of fire prevention strategies, emergency preparedness planning and staff training. The course will also examine the critical role of insurance to protect your dental practice including property coverage, business interruption and equipment replacement.

2. Navigating Insurance Company Audits, Complaints, and Investigations

Speaker: Dr. Chris Swayze
3 CE points

3. An Introduction to Dental 3D Printing

Speaker: Dr. Matthew Chuk
1 CE points

Course Description

Dental 3D printing is a cutting-edge technology that allows for the creation of custom dental solutions with high precision. Using digital models, it can produce crowns, bridges, implants, dentures and orthodontic devices. This process enhances treatment accuracy, reduces production time and minimizes waste. It enables faster, more personalized care for patients, improving both comfort and functionality. Dental 3D printing is revolutionizing the dental industry, making procedures more efficient and cost-effective, a benefit to both the patient and the practice. This course will introduce the basic concepts of 3D printing and some easy, cost-effective applications such as the fabrication of study models and bruxism appliances. A live demonstration of capturing patient data, designing dental appliances and getting it ready for 3D printing will be performed.

MBA, DDS, HBSc

With an extensive background in dental regulation and professional development, Dr. Swayze oversees the Continuing Dental Education program at the Faculty of Dentistry. He is a member of the CSA Technical Committee on Medical Device Reprocessing (Z314) [Z262 TC], setting the national standard on reprocessing, and a member of IPAC Canada.
